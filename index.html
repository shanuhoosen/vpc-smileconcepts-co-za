Meet Dr Tasha

Passionate About Your Smile

Dr Tasha Pillay

BDS (UWC) · PG Dip Interceptive Orthodontics

A born and bred Durbanite, Dr Tasha obtained her Bachelor in Dental Surgery from the University of the Western Cape in 2013. After community service at Addington Hospital and serving communities in Phoenix, Durban North and Umhlanga, she established her own practice in 2018 at the Kings Park precinct.

Characterised by her warm, friendly attitude and constant need to care for her patients, Dr Tasha specialises in paediatric dentistry and cosmetic work. She holds a post-graduate diploma in interceptive orthodontics and is currently pursuing her second diploma in paediatrics.
